About our resort

Nestled in the tranquil Tirthan Valley, our resort offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and luxury. Surrounded by lush green forests and situated alongside the sparkling Tirthan River, our resort is the ultimate destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Our rooms and suites are tastefully designed with comfort and relaxation in mind, featuring large windows that offer stunning views of the valley.
Each room is equipped with modern amenities. Our resort also features a variety of dining options, including a fine-dining restaurant that serves delicious local and international cuisine. Our open-air terrace restaurant offers the perfect place to enjoy a meal while taking in the stunning views of the Tirthan Valley.
Come and experience the beauty and tranquility of the Tirthan Valley at our resort. We look forward to welcoming you soon!
